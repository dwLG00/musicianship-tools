How to Play
  • Guess the notes played by selecting the correct keys on the keyboard
  • The lowest (root) note is automatically selected
  • If you need to listen to the notes again, click Play Notes Again
  • You can choose between Chord and Arpeggio playback
  • Have fun and good luck!
